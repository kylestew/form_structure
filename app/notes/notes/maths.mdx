Lift ($L$) can be determined by the Lift Coefficient ($C_L$) using the following equation:

$$
L = \frac \rho v^2 S C_L
$$

Another classic equation:

$$
a^2 + b^2 = c^2
$$

-   $a$ = side of right triangle
-   $b$ = side of right triangle
-   $c$ = hypotenuse
